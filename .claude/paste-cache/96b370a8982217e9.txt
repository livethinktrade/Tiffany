Background:
Analyzing user engagement within Facebook groups during the first half of 2023 provides key insights beneficial to group administrators and the platform. Your role as a data analyst is to extract these valuable metrics surrounding user activity in these groups.

Question: 
Write a query to calculate the percentage of posts that received at least one reaction within the first 3 days of being posted for each group during the first half of 2023. Only consider groups with more than 15 posts. Order the groups based on this percentage in descending order.
